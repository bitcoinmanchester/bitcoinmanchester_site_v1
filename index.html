What?

Bitcoin Manchester is a meetup for Bitcoin users, miners, buyers, sellers or anyone else otherwise interested in Bitcoin in the north-west.

Where?

Bitcoin Manchester is hosted at the MadLab on Edge Street in the Northern Quarter.

When?

We meet regularly on the first Monday of each month (unless announced otherwise). Come along!

Join the conversation

Introduce yourself on the mailing list: bitcoin-manchester (also sign up here if you want to receive event announcements via email or Google Groups).
